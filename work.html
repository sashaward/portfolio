How I do things

Identify what the problem is.  Get everyone on board.  Prototype.  Get it wrong.  Get it less wrong. Keep testing it. Do it all over again.

My expertise

Defining the problem

Identify what the real problem is by running experiments to prove or disprove early assumptions.

Facilitating & Iterating

Orchestrate productive conversations between people of different disciplines and backgrounds in order to rapidly generate a range of ideas.

Sketching & Prototyping

Visualise solutions by using a range of low and high fidelity methods, whatever's necessary for the job.

Testing & Adapting

Getting out of the building and testing designs on real people. Continually injecting the feedback into the design.

Delivering

Pairing with developers in order to communicate and share why the design is the way it is and how it should function. Because this is what actually matters.
