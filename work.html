How I do things

Identify what the problem is.  Get everyone on board.  Prototype.  Get it wrong.  Get it less wrong. Keep testing it. Do it all over again.

My expertise

Defining the problem

Identify what the real problem is by running experiments to prove or disprove early assumptions. It's a collaborative exercise that relies on strong communication and a curious mindset.

Facilitating & Iterating

Orchestrate productive conversations between people of different disciplines and backgrounds in order to rapidly generate a range of ideas and reach group consensus.

Sketching & Prototyping

Visualise solutions by using a range of low and high fidelity methods, whatever's necessary for the job (im not tool dogmatic).

Testing & Adapting

Getting out of the building and testing designs on real people. Continually injecting the feedback into the design.

Delivering

Pairing with developers in order to communicate and share why the design is the way it is and how it should function, because ultimately this is what matters.
